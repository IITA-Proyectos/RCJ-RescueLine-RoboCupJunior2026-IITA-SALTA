**Mentors:**

- **Mr. Gustavo Viollaz**: 
He is the team director, and he helps with delivering or ordering the needed parts for our robot. He also gives good advice and tips on how to assemble the robot. Additionally, our director also gives us directions when needed.
- **Mr. Enzo Juarez**:
He is the team mentor and has been involved in our robotics projects for several years. He is an electronic technician, and his specialty is programming. He always helps us when we have problems.

**Members:**
